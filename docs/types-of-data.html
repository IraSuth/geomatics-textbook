2.1.1 Nominal

Just a name/category, no ranking or direction. * Plant species * Zoning category * Language spoken at home

2.1.2 Ordinal

More than a name, some sort of ranking, but no numerical scale/meaning, you cant take differences, * Relative sizes (short, medium, tall) * Relative ages (young/old) * Spice level
